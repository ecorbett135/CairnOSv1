# CairnOS Logo Direction

## Hiker Logix Reference

The local Hiker Logix assets use a deep cool-blue field, a pale sky-blue circular mountain badge, a heavy white uppercase wordmark, and a lighter spaced tagline. The feel is optimistic and outdoor-oriented, with enough simplicity to work as an app icon.

## Recommended CairnOS Direction

Use CairnOS as the planning/reasoning sibling to Hiker Logix, not a clone of it.

- Keep the cool blue palette so the products feel related.
- Replace the hiker/mountain-action symbol with a cairn stack, route line, and contour geometry.
- Use `CAIRN` in white and `OS` in pale blue to make the platform/engine role readable.
- Prefer the tagline `EXPEDITION PLANNING ENGINE` for public/product usage.
- Avoid a safety-authority posture in the mark; the logo should feel like planning software, not rescue/navigation infrastructure.

## Optional Two-Brand Refresh

If Hiker Logix is updated later, keep the shared badge grammar but differentiate the roles:

- Hiker Logix: field execution, actuals, personal logs, mobile companion.
- CairnOS: planning, feasibility, route reasoning, exports, calibration.

That would let both products share the blue outdoor system while the icons carry different jobs: Hiker Logix remains hiker/mountain-forward, CairnOS becomes cairn/route/terrain-forward.
